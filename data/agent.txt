Project Name

Retail Operations Agent

A central AI system for a retail chain with many stores across India.

It answers questions like:

Which Hyderabad stores may run out of milk next week?

and internally:

checks inventory → checks sales → forecasts demand → calculates reorder → gives recommendation
1. What problem are we solving?

Retail companies have many stores. Each store has:

sales data
inventory data
supplier data
product data
policies

Managers manually check dashboards to find:

low stock
stock-out risk
fast-moving products
reorder quantity
policy rules

Our AI agent becomes the central operations manager.

9. What each tool does
Inventory Tool

Reads inventory and finds current stock.

Input: city/product/category
Output: current stock, reorder point, low stock flag
Sales Tool

Calculates recent sales velocity.

Input: store_id/product_id
Output: average daily sales
Forecast Tool

Predicts next 7-day demand.

average_daily_sales × 7
Reorder Tool

Calculates recommended reorder.

recommended_reorder = forecast_demand - current_stock + safety_stock
RAG Tool

Answers policy questions from company documents.
