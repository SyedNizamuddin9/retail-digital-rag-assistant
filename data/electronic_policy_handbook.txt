

ELECTRONICS RETURN AND REPLACEMENT POLICY
Mobile phones are eligible for replacement within 7 days from the date of delivery if customers identify manufacturing defects, hardware failures, or software issues not caused by physical damage. Customers must raise the request through the support portal and provide invoice details, product serial number, and issue description.
Laptops, televisions, refrigerators, and washing machines are eligible only for replacement and not refund after successful delivery and installation. Replacement requests are approved only after technical verification. Products damaged due to customer misuse, voltage fluctuations, liquid damage, or unauthorized repairs are not eligible.
Products damaged during delivery must be reported within 48 hours along with mandatory unboxing images and videos. The quality verification team may inspect the product before approving replacement. Products without original packaging, accessories, invoice, or serial number labels may not qualify.

REFUND POLICY
Refunds for prepaid orders are processed within 5 to 7 business days after return approval. Refund timelines for EMI purchases may vary depending on the issuing bank and payment gateway processing timelines. Cash-on-delivery orders are refunded directly to customer bank accounts after verification.
Refund timelines may vary for prepaid cancellations, return pickups, and failed payment settlements depending on banking workflows, verification checks, and payment gateway processing timelines.
Refunds are not applicable for products damaged due to customer misuse, accidental damage, liquid damage, voltage fluctuations, or unauthorized repairs. Products purchased during flash sales, limited-time promotional events, or clearance sales may have restricted refund eligibility.

WARRANTY POLICY
Apple mobile devices include a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware manufacturing defects. Samsung televisions include 2 years comprehensive warranty coverage and additional panel warranty for selected premium models. Laptop warranties generally cover motherboard, processor, RAM, and internal hardware defects.
Warranty claims require original invoice, serial number verification, and valid warranty documentation. Physical damage, screen cracks, dents, water damage, and third-party repairs void manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty plans are available for selected premium electronics and appliances.

DISCOUNT AND CASHBACK POLICY
HDFC Bank credit card holders receive 10 percent instant discount during festival campaigns and seasonal sales. Premium members are eligible for additional cashback offers on electronics purchases above 50000 INR. No-cost EMI is available on selected laptops, smartphones, televisions, and appliances for up to 12 months.
Exchange bonus offers apply only after successful pickup and verification of old devices. Discounts, cashback offers, and exchange bonuses cannot be combined unless explicitly specified during campaigns.

DELIVERY AND SHIPPING POLICY
Electronics products are usually delivered within 3 to 5 business days depending on delivery location and seller availability. Large appliances may require additional installation scheduling after successful delivery. Same-day delivery is available only for selected products and premium members in metro cities.
Customers can track orders through the website and mobile application order tracking section. Installation support for televisions and large appliances is coordinated separately after delivery confirmation.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY
Customer support services are available daily from 8 AM to 10 PM including weekends and public holidays. Customers can raise support tickets through the mobile application, website support center, or customer care hotline.
Escalation requests are reviewed by specialized support teams for high-value electronics and warranty disputes. Replacement requests may require image uploads, diagnostic reports, or technician verification depending on the product category.

PAYMENT REVERSAL POLICY
Payment reversal applies when a transaction is charged but the order is not successfully created. In such cases, the amount is automatically reversed to the original payment method within 3 to 5 business days. This is not treated as a product refund because no delivered item is involved.
Customers sometimes confuse payment reversals with refund settlements because both involve money being credited back to prepaid payment methods. Payment reversals occur before order confirmation, while refunds happen after successful order placement and return approval.

PROMOTIONAL CASHBACK SETTLEMENT POLICY
Cashback for promotional campaigns is credited within 7 to 10 business days after order delivery. Cashback delays may occur if the payment provider requires additional validation. Cashback is different from refunds and cannot be requested as direct bank transfer.
Customers sometimes mistake cashback settlement timelines for refund processing timelines because both involve payment credits after order completion.

ORDER CANCELLATION REFUND POLICY
If an order is cancelled before shipping, prepaid payments are refunded within 3 to 5 business days. If cancellation is attempted after shipping, the refund timeline depends on return pickup and quality verification.
Refund timelines for cancelled prepaid orders are generally shorter than product return refunds because product quality verification is not required before shipping.

FAILED EMI TRANSACTION POLICY
If an EMI transaction fails but the customer account is debited, the bank usually reverses the amount within 5 to 7 business days. EMI reversal timelines depend on the issuing bank and are separate from product return refunds.
EMI reversal timelines may appear similar to refund timelines, but EMI reversals are processed directly between the issuing bank and payment gateway systems instead of return management workflows.
